Devin Incerti

I am a PhD candidate in political economy at the Woodrow Wilson School of Public and International Affairs at Princeton University expecting to defend my dissertation this September. I am an interdisciplinary social scientist with methodological training in microeconomics and statistics. I enjoy use quantitative models to solve interesting applied problems in a number of areas including public policy, politics, business, sports, and health. My current work focuses on topics in health services research and political economy.

In my pre-PhD life, I was a rather unsuccessful collegiate baseball player whose career was cut short by a shoulder injury. I still enjoy trying to relive my days as an athlete by playing sports when my shoulder permits (usually basketball). I also somehow enjoy lifting up heavy objects and then putting them back down.
